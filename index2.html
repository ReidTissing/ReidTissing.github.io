WHAT IS THIS AND WHO ARE YOU ?

My name is Reid Tissing. I make art and code for work and fun, like a horoscope generator and some other things.

Horrorscope

I made a horoscope generator. It scrapes several popular online horoscope publications and uses Markov chains to generate a somewhat-coherent horoscope based on what word or phrase usually follows one another. It scrapes using node.js and does the parsing/display with python/flask.

Emplace

I developed a front-to-back end web application that acts as a social network during natural disasters. Individual users can take photos with a description, the geotag is extracted and mapped so others can see what's happening and where.
Used: AWS, Python, Flask, Google Maps API, SQL.

Drone Mapping

I did plant-growth as well as 3D mapping of local areas using a Phantom 3 Advanced drone. The 3D models were created using dronedeploy and blender for texture mapping/displacement.

Traffic Cam Timelapse

By scraping several 'traffic cameras' at regular intervals with python and ffmpeg, then running a custom photoshop script, I overlayed them into a higher-resolution look at cities throughout the day.

FoxBlend

I automated the process of capturing of frames from a morning talk show, then ran the difference layers through photoshop. This is the result.
